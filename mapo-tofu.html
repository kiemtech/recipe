Kung Pao Chicken

Mapo Tofu

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs ground pork
  • 1" knob Ginger (miced)
  • 4 cloves garlic (miced)
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 tbsp doubanjang
  • 2 tsp Sichuan Chilli flakes
  • 1-2 tsp Sichuan peppercorn power
  • 2 tbsp Shoaxing Wine
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 2/3 cup chickecn/beef stock
  • 1 lb soft tofu
  • 2 tsp cornstrach + 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp seamem oil

Instruction

  • Mince ginger & garlic
  • Slice white portion of scallions
  • Chops the green part of scallions for garnish
  • Drain & cut tofu into cubes. Simmer tofu in salted water for 3 minutes and set aside
  • Brown galic and ginger. Add ground meat
  • Add all seasonings except Sesame Oil & Green Onions
  • Add stocking & tofu. Bring to boil
  • Pour cornstrach & mix well. Add sesame oil and green onions
