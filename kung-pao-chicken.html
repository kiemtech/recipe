Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, large dice
  • 1 red bell pepper, large dice
  • 1 green bell pepper, large dice
  • 3 celery stalks, large dice
  • 2 small carrots, large dice
  • 5 oz water chestnuts, drained & thinkly sliced
  • 4 - 5 garlic cloves, miced
  • 1/2 cup roasted unsalted peanuts
  • 1 lb chicken, thinly sliced
Chicken Seasonings
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp white or black peppercorn
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
Cooking Sauce
  • 3 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp distilled vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sambal chili paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp chicken bouilon
  • 1 tbsp cornstrach & 2 tbsp water (mixed well)

Instruction

  • Large diced onion, bell peppers, celery, carrots
  • Thinkly slice chicken & marinate with Checken Seasonings
  • Mixed Cooking Sauce
  • Saute the garlic & add chicken. Set aside
  • Add onions, dried chillies and rest of veggies
  • Pour the sauce & the chicken back. Add water chestnuts & peanuts
